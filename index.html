About Me

my name is Diane Stefanie Delgado, and I am someone who is always trying to grow and improve myself. I believe that every day is an opportunity to learn something new, whether it’s from school, experiences, or the people around me. I may not be perfect, but I always try my best in everything that I do.

as a person, I would describe myself as kind, understanding, and a little bit shy at first. I may not always speak a lot when I’m around new people, but once I get comfortable, I can be fun and easy to be with. I also care deeply about my friends and family, and I always try to support them whenever they need me.

I am also an only child, which has shaped who I am today. growing up without siblings taught me how to be independent and responsible at a young age. I learned how to entertain myself and solve my own problems most of the time. at the same time, I developed a strong and close bond with my parents, and I truly appreciate everything they have done for me.

I am someone who loves cooking, and it is one of the things that I truly enjoy doing in my free time. Cooking is my therapy because it helps me relax and forget about stress for a while. I like trying out simple recipes and learning new ways to prepare food. It makes me feel calm and creative, and I feel happy when I can share the food I make with my family.

I also have dreams and goals that I want to achieve someday. I want to have a stable life, a successful career, and make my parents proud. I know that the road will not always be easy, but I am willing to work hard and stay focused on my goals.

My Hobbies

Cooking is one of my favorite hobbies because it allows me to be creative while making something meaningful and enjoyable. I love experimenting with different ingredients, flavors, and techniques, whether I’m trying out new recipes or adding my own twist to familiar dishes. The process of preparing food is both relaxing and rewarding, especially when I see others enjoy what I’ve made. Cooking also gives me a chance to learn about different cultures through their cuisines, expanding my appreciation for food around the world. Overall, it’s more than just a hobby—it’s a passion that brings me joy and satisfaction.
